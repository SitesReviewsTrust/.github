## ℹ️ About

**Sites.Reviews** ([sites.reviews](https://sites.reviews)) is an independent catalog of companies and websites with user reviews and trust ratings — helping people decide who to trust online, and giving businesses a place to build reputation. Check any site before you trust or pay it: **trust score, real reviews, and security/scam checks.**
